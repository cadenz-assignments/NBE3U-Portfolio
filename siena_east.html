Indigenous Influencer

Siena East

Her Background

Siena East is a Choctaw writer, comedian, actress, and director based in Los Angeles. She was born on September 8th, 1993, making her 29 years old as of May 2023.

Siena East had her university education at New York University with a degree in Fine Arts - Film and Television production. During which, she produced her first short film "Lycanphobia." Which was recognized and won several awards at film festivals.

Siena East was also selected for programs such as the LA Skins Television Writer's Lab and NAMA's Native American Showrunner program, where she was mentored by professionals the field.

Her Career

Siena East has worked as an acting, directing assistant, model, and teacher's assistant in Digital Art Creation and Music Video Creation at Marlborough School. She also worked as a substitute teacher, post-production and office production assistant at Warner Bros. She is now working at FOX Corporation as Executive Story Editor and Co-Producer.

In general, Siena East creates media content such as TV shows, short movies, and stand-up comedy talk shows. More specifically, she has performed at various venues and events, including the Hollywood improv, Association of American Indian Affairs, and Good Medicine at Oakland Museum.

Siena East is currently working as the Co-Producer at Fox Corporation, which is a fairly large corporation with 9000 annual employees as of 2020. She is also part of a DND youtube channel named NDND. It is created by a group of Indigenous DND players. This is a breath of fresh air to the DND community, as most of them are not composed of a diverse group of players.

Even though Siena East does not state the reason why she is in the film making line of work, I believe Siena East is doing it because she is passionate about her work. She has been creating media since 2012.

Reflection

Siena East seems really dedicated to what she does - creating media pieces including films, shows, and performing. I watched some of the stand-up shows on her youtube channel and they were entertaining. Also, as a DND player myself, her participation in DND interested me, and made me want to learn more about what she does and who she is.

I believe that passionate work makes good work. If someone is passionate about what they do, they can do a better job than someone who isn't passionate about their work. Siena East shows a significant amount of passion in creating media pieces in the form of short films, writing, comedy, etc. I believe that Indigenous writers are one of the most under-represented groups of writers in modern TV/movie production and Siena East is a talented TV writer and has her own ideas.
