2. Text Anthology

3. Food Sovereignty

This is the infographic I've created for Day 2 of the Cottagers and Indians play study. Initially, I was dissatisfied with the first version of the infographic I created and submitted for feedback. However, for the purpose of this portfolio, I took the opportunity to completely remake it while considering the feedback I received on the original product. I think that my efforts have paid off, as the revamped infographic is now much more visually engaging and appealing compared to the original version.

During the process of recreating the infographic, I made an effort to make the infographic more engaging for the audience and more attention grabbing. For this reason, I included a big eye-catching number in the font center of the infographic displaying some statistics. I think the new version really nicely presents the organization. The new version also leaves out the less important parts of the information, making the infographic less cluttered and easier to look at.

This experience of revisiting and improving upon my initial work demonstrates my dedication to continuous learning and growth as a creator. By acknowledging and incorporating feedback, I was able to enhance the quality and effectiveness of my infographic. This process showcases my ability to adapt, learn from constructive criticism, and transform a project into a more polished and impactful final product.
